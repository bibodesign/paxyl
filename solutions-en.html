≤ Hosted Mode (XCloud)

The popularity of the Cloud results directly from bringing the best of the two worlds: the simplicity of the Cloud and the focus on the specific needs of each organisation. Paxyl has therefore put together an XCloud offer, providing all the required solutions for the management and operation of an efficient contact center.

Scalable, flexible and secure, the XCloud Solution allows you to improve your customers’ paths due to more personal and homogeneous experiences on all contact points. Mobile, SMS, Social Networks… Paxyl Solutions, based on the Genesys Technology, will adjust perfectly to the preferences of your customers. With the Paxyl Cloud Contact Center Solution, your enterprise will achieve its goals quickly and that –in all flexibility.

Our platform and data are hosted in 4Degree data centers (Vidéotron) on high-availability, fully secure servers powered by VCloud Director of Micrologic Cirrus project. In most cases, your existing applications could be integrated into the XCloud, in a hybrid configuration. In this way, you will operate your transition in a flexible, transparent way.

By choosing to migrate your contact center to XCloud, your enterprise will considerably reduce its costs and the “total cost of ownership” (TCO). Speaking about agility, a Hosted Model accelerates the transformation of business lines and improves customer experience – two powerful levers on the global market where a contact center serves as a fundamental differentiation factor for the enterprises, no matter their size and the sector of their activity.

The unique experience of Paxyl, combined with the power of the world leader Genesys Application, allows us to offer you one of the most interesting solutions on the market.

  • A robust, stable solution, hosted in Québec in 4Degrees Data Centers (Vidéotron);
  • A Tier III and PCI-DSS Certified Data Center;
  • A flexible architecture to allow the customers follow their own evolution;
  • Applications with an interface in either English or French;
  • A solution integrating the main CRM software;
  • A solution integrating the main telephone systems;
  • Customizable “Agent Desktop Applications" according to your needs;
  • Considerable cost and effort reduction for deployment and updating.
